Built for
Nairobi Events

Grivio Events KE was born out of a simple frustration: planning events in Nairobi is hard. Finding reliable vendors, comparing quotes, managing follow-ups — it's a full-time job.

We built Grivio to change that. We're a professional middleman that connects clients with a hand-picked network of trusted vendors — so the right people always find each other.

Whether you're a client with a vision or a vendor wanting more bookings, Grivio is your partner.
